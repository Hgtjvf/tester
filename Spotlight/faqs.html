Frequently Asked Questions

Have a question? You might find the answer here. If not, feel free to contact us!

General Platform Information

What is PerkView?

PerkView is a platform where users can watch videos, earn rewards, and engage with content through interactive features.

How does PerkView work?

Users can watch videos, participate in challenges, earn points, and redeem them for rewards.

Sign-Up/Account Related

How do I create an account?

Simply click on the “Sign Up” button, provide your details, and create an account to get started.

I forgot my password. How can I reset it?

Click on “Forgot Password?” on the login page, enter your email address, and follow the instructions to reset your password.

Can I change my username or email address?

Yes, you can modify your username or email from the account settings page.

Earning Rewards

How do I earn rewards?

You can earn rewards by watching videos, participating in challenges, and completing other platform activities.

How do I redeem my rewards?

You can redeem your rewards through the Rewards section on your account page. Choose from a variety of prizes or benefits.

What are daily rewards?

Daily rewards are bonus points or benefits that you can claim every day by logging into the platform.

Video Uploading & Content Creation

How can I upload a video?

Click on the “Upload Video” button in the account section, choose your video, and follow the prompts to submit it for review.

What types of videos are allowed on PerkView?

We allow videos that comply with our community guidelines, including family-friendly content and original works.

How do I monetize my videos?

You can monetize your videos through ad revenue sharing or tips from viewers if your account is eligible for monetization.

Gamification & Challenges

What are the challenges and contests?

Challenges are tasks or activities that you can complete to earn points and rewards. Contests may have specific themes and prizes.

How do I track my progress in challenges?

You can track your progress in the Challenges section of your profile, where it shows your completion status and remaining tasks.

Platform Features & Navigation

How do I use the search feature?

Use the search bar at the top of the page to search for videos, users, or keywords to find relevant content.

How do I interact with videos (like, comment, share)?

You can like, comment, or share videos by clicking on the respective icons below the video player.

Payment & Account

How can I add money to my wallet or make a purchase?

You can add funds by going to your account’s wallet section and choosing your preferred payment method.

How can I withdraw my rewards or points?

To withdraw, go to the Rewards section and choose the withdrawal option. You can redeem rewards for cash, gift cards, or other benefits.

Support & Reporting Issues

I have a technical issue with the platform. What should I do?

If you face any technical issues, contact our customer support team via the contact form or email us at support@perkview.com.

How do I report inappropriate content?

Click on the “Report” button next to any content that violates our community guidelines, and it will be reviewed by our team.

Account Deletion or Cancellation

How can I delete my account?

To delete your account, go to your account settings, click on “Delete Account,” and follow the instructions.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes, if you have a subscription, you can cancel it in the subscription settings of your account.
